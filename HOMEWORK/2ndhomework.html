Our Books

  • Life of Pi -Yann Martel
    •

    Life of Pi by Yann Martel is an adventurous and philosophical novel about a young boy named Pi Patel who survives a shipwreck. Stranded on a lifeboat in the Pacific Ocean, he must share it with a Bengal tiger named Richard Parker. The story explores faith, survival, and the power of storytelling, as Pi faces the vast sea and the struggle between hope and despair.
